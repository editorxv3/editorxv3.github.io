I'm Ayaan, a freelance video editor based in Istanbul. For the past three years I've cut travel and lifestyle content for social platforms, edited broadcast segments for TV news reporters, and I currently edit an ongoing Pakistani drama series.

Growing up between Pakistani and Turkish culture — and working fluently across English, Turkish, Urdu, and Arabic — I edit for audiences on both sides of a story, which matters when the client is a channel, a tourism board, or a reporter filing from the field.
